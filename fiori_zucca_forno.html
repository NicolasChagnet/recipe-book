Fiori di zucca al forno

  • 12 servings
  • 30 min
  • Source
  • Salty

Calories 116 kcal · Fats 9.2 g · Saturated fats 4.49 g · Carbs 2.7 g · Sugar 1.5 g · Fiber 0.4 g · Protein 5.5 g · Salt 0.186 g

  • 12 zucchini flowers
  • 20g bread crumbs
  • 20g Pecorino
  • 2 eggs, beaten

Mix the bread crumbs and Pecorino; dip the flowers in eggs and the bread mixture before laying on a baking tray. Put in the oven for 5min at 200°C.

Enjoy!
