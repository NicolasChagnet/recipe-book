* `1 tbsp` flour
* `1 tbsp` yoghurt
* `1` egg
* `1 tbsp` sugar
* `1 tbsp` yeast
* `1 tsp` neutral oil
* `2 squares` chocolate (optional)

> Mix in a mug and put in the microwave for 2-3 mins.
