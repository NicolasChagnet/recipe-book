* `480 mL`  oats
* `720 mL`  milk (almond e.g.)
* `3 tbsp`  peanut butter

> Mix in a jar until combined.

---

* `6 tbsp`  chia seeds
* `¼ tsp` ground cinnamon
* Fruits, toppings to taste

> Add toppings and gently mix.

---

> Keep the jar in the fridge overnight.
